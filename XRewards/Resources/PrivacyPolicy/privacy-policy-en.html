XRewards Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

XRewards ("we") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in our member rewards platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address, sign-in method (email, Google, Apple), and Firebase user ID.
  • Member profile: display name, member ID, and join date.
  • Rewards data: point balances, referrals, activity/transactions, dividend estimates, and team statistics.
  • Guest preview: if you use guest mode, Firebase anonymous sign-in is used for preview only — no real points are saved.
  • Device & security signals: information processed through Firebase App Check and related services to prevent abuse.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide sign-in, registration, guest preview, and account management;
  • Record referrals, points, dividends, and team features;
  • Protect platform security and prevent fraud or unauthorized access;
  • Improve the product and troubleshoot issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use the following providers, processing data only as needed to deliver the service:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, App Check)
  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign In

4. Data Storage & Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including access controls, encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), and App Check validation. Member data is primarily stored on Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure.

5. Your Rights

  • Review this Privacy Policy in the app;
  • Edit your display name in Profile;
  • Permanently delete your account and associated data via Delete Account (subject to legal requirements). After deletion, your points, referrals, and dividend history cannot be recovered.

6. Children's Privacy

XRewards is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we collected a child's information without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy and publish changes in the app or on our website. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

Privacy questions: support@xrewards.app
